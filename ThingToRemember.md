#### CIDR vs VLSM: 
There is a debate about this:
+ Some people will say:
	+ CIDR is just a notation of subnet mask in decimal number. 
	+ VLSM is Subnetting. I
+ Other will say:
	+  CIDR is Supernetting. 
	+  VLSM is Subnetting.
